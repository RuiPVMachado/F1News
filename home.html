F1 Season Overview

The 2024 Formula 1 season promises to be one of the most exciting yet, with 24 races scheduled across 21 countries. Defending champion Max Verstappen faces stiff competition from Lewis Hamilton, Charles Leclerc, and a host of talented drivers vying for the title.

With new technical regulations in place and teams pushing the boundaries of innovation, this season is set to deliver thrilling races and unexpected twists. Stay tuned for all the latest updates and analysis as the drama unfolds on and off the track.
